Why MediHelp24?

What are MediHelp24 sevices?

We are committed to help the senior citizens of the society and help them to face old age challenges. Following are the services initiated as per the preferences given in the registration form.

  1. Medicine delivery on call

    2. This includes MediSevak visiting you at the scheduled time, collecting the prescription and other essentials order and delivering at the earliest. Maximum time taken would be 24hours

  2. Medicine remainders on call

    3. The MediHelp24 care coordinator will give you regular call depending upon your schedule to remind you for medicine intake. The coordinator will also maintain a chart record of calls and response received from the end user.

  3. Medicine refill

    4. You may indicate the regularly consumed medicine to the MediHelp24 coordinator and the MediSevak will visit you two days before the medicine gets exhausted.

  4. Special day

    5. MediHelp24 will send you bouquet and wishes on special events like Birthday and Marriage anniversaries etc.

  5. Regular personal visit

    6. This service is available on special request and is chargeable extra wherein MediHelp24 care coordinator or MediSevak will personally visit on regular interval like Alternate day or Weekly or Fortnightly and keep the visit details updated to their registered primary contacts.

Frequently Asked Questions?

  1. What MediSevak does?

    2. MediSevak are the trained agents from MediHelp24 who have basic understanding of home nursing and help in delivering medicines and other services to the elderly member.

  2. Who are eligible for MediHelp24 services?

    3. We serve only registered members and general calls are not entertained. The registered members are identified either by registered mobile number (RMN) or Member registration number generated at the time of registration.

  3. How can I get registered to MediHelp24 services?

    4. The registration can be done online and in offline mode also. Online you need to fill some details and upload your address and ID proof. The form goes for verification and on successful verification the MediHelp24 Member ID is generated. In offline mode you need to call customer support and schedule an appointment. Our Care coordinator would assist and do the registration formalities at your residence.

  4. What are the charges involved in availing MediHelp24 services?

    5. The service model is based more on quality of service and support to elders than high volume and high profit oriented businesses. As itâ€™s a limited registered customer model we charge minimum support fees from members so that the business is sustained and we continue to give support service to the members. The yearly fee for basic membership is 1000/- which provides the basic delivery services to member. Other additional services are charges extra as per the customerâ€™s preference for availing the service.

  5. What is the mode of payment for delivery of medicine or other daily essentials?

    6. The MediSevak picks up the prescription and purchases medicine from the nearest MediHelp24 registered pharmacy. On delivery the payment can be done in Cash or by card depending upon the convenience of the customer

  6. What are the delivery charges and is there any minimum order value to serve?

    7. Yes, we have fixed delivery charges of Rs 60/- per order delivered. Which is billed separately by the MediSevak and receipt is taken on the same as proof of stock delivered. There is no minimum order value.

  7. What if I return or cancel the order?

    8. We accept cancellation of order but no return policy (ref: Return and Cancellation policy). On the event of cancellation after the order is placed the delivery charges is chargeable.
